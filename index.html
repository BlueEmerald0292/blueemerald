About the Project

Blue Emerald is a premium commercial project in Wanowrie, Pune. Built with over 30 years of experience, it features only 15 luxurious offices spread across 7 floors on a prime plot of land. Customizable workspaces, smart planning, and great amenities define this modern space.

Architecture

Designed by Envision Studio with 15+ years of experience, Blue Emerald features curved glass facades, thoughtful planning, and artistic structure — making it a landmark in Pune's skyline.

Specifications

  • Earthquake Resistant R.C.C Frame
  • Vitrified Tiles Flooring in all Offices
  • Concealed Copper Wiring, Superior Plumbing
  • Sliding Aluminium Windows with M.S. Grills
  • Anti-skid Tiles in Toilets & Terraces
  • Modern Sanitary Ware & Chrome Fittings

Location & Connectivity

Strategically located at Wanowrie junction — connected to Fatima Nagar, Salunke Vihar & NIBM. Close to malls, hospitals, gyms, showrooms, and public transport. A prime destination for your growing business.
