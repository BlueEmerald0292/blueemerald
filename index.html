About the Project

Blue Emerald is a high-end commercial project situated at the center of Wanowrie. Combining contemporary architecture, functional design, and premium finishes — it's where your business finds its best expression.

Architecture

Designed by leading architectural firm Envision Studio, the structure balances luxury and practicality with its signature curved glass façade and open-plan flexibility.

Specifications

  • Earthquake Resistant RCC Frame Structure
  • High-speed Elevators & Fire Fighting System
  • Smart Cabling and Concealed Wiring
  • Designer Lobby & Vitrified Tile Flooring
  • Anti-skid Tiles in Toilets
  • Energy-efficient LED Lighting

Location & Connectivity

Strategically located at Wanowrie Junction, Blue Emerald offers seamless access to Fatima Nagar, MG Road, NIBM and Salunke Vihar. Close to retail malls, hospitals, showrooms, and public transport.
