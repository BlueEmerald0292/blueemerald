About the Project

Blue Emerald is a premium commercial development in Wanowrie, Pune. With only 15 exclusive offices across 7 floors, the project stands tall with landmark architecture, advanced facilities, and a rooftop terrace to elevate your business identity.

Architecture

Designed by Envision Studio, Blue Emerald features stunning curved glass facades, smart layouts, and an urban design language that blends form with functionality.

Specifications

  • Earthquake Resistant RCC Frame Structure
  • Modern Elevators & Fire Systems
  • Concealed Copper Wiring
  • Anti-skid Flooring in Wet Areas
  • Vitrified Tile Flooring
  • Premium Glass Facades & Lighting

Location & Connectivity

Located at Wanowrie Junction, Blue Emerald connects seamlessly to Fatima Nagar, NIBM, MG Road, and Salunke Vihar. Close to top schools, hospitals, malls, and showrooms.
